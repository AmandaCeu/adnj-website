Welcome to OABF 2025

The Africa Diaspora Network in Japan (ADNJ) is pleased to welcome you to the Osaka Africa Business Forum 2025 (OABF), to be held on August 25 at the Higashiosaka Culture Creation Center.

Now in its 4th edition, OABF 2025 is held under the inspiring theme "Carving the Future with Africa." This year marks a new milestone as we move beyond dialogue to co-creation...

Since the launch of the Discover Africa initiative in 2020, ADNJ has worked to deepen mutual understanding and unlock collaborative potential between African and Japanese enterprises. Following two years of successful forums centered on discovering and connecting with Africa, we are now carving new paths together with concrete business opportunities.

By participating as a sponsor, partner, or volunteer, you will help strengthen a vibrant ecosystem that empowers innovation, entrepreneurship, and trust across borders.

Join us in building this future, with Africa.

Carving the Future Together

The Osaka Africa Business Forum 2025 embraces the theme "Carving the Future with Africa", with a special focus on the role of youth and the potential of equal, collaborative partnerships. In a time of global uncertainty and transformation, this forum calls for a shift from one-sided aid models toward mutual engagement...

Africa today is not merely a recipient of development assistance, it is a dynamic continent rich in culture, creativity, and youthful energy. Its growing population, innovative spirit, and abundant resources make it a crucial partner in building a sustainable and inclusive global future. At the same time, Japan brings long-standing experience, advanced technologies, and a cultural ethic of precision and responsibility. The intersection of these complementary strengths offers fertile ground for long-term collaboration.

At the heart of this year's forum is the belief that youth-led co-creation will shape the next century. It is the young generation, both African and Japanese, who must engage directly with each other, exchange ideas, and foster mutual understanding to unlock new possibilities. Whether through entrepreneurship, education, technology, or cultural exchange, youth are not only future leaders, they are present-day changemakers.

This forum aims to provide a platform for emerging leaders, entrepreneurs, and creative thinkers from both regions to connect, collaborate, and envision a shared future. Through dialogue sessions, networking opportunities, and showcase events, participants will explore how trust-based partnerships can go beyond transactional business and foster deeper human connections and social innovation.

Now is the time to redefine Africa-Japan relations, not as donor and recipient, but as equal partners engaged in shaping a common destiny. The Osaka Africa Business Forum 2025 aspires to become a milestone event that brings together voices across sectors and generations and lays the groundwork for a future built not only on commerce, but on empathy, respect, and co-creation.

Let this be the moment where new bridges are built, and where today's youth take a bold step toward a shared and sustainable tomorrow, with Africa, not just for Africa.
